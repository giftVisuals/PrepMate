The problem

Most revision is a guessing game

Textbooks and past questions don't know what YOU personally struggle with. So you end up re-reading chapters you already understand, while your actual weak spots stay untouched until exam day.

📚
Generic past questions treat every student the same — no matter what you've already mastered.
⏳
Limited study time gets wasted on topics you're already strong in, just because they're next in the textbook.
❓
Wrong answers in most apps just show a red X — with no explanation of why, or how to actually fix the gap.
💸
Private tutors who could personalize your prep are expensive and out of reach for most students.
The solution

How PrepMate works

Four simple steps, and it gets sharper about you every single time.

1
Pick a subject & topic
Chemistry, Biology, Physics, Maths, English — whatever you're prepping for. PrepMate's AI generates fresh, exam-style practice questions on the spot, not from a fixed question bank.
2
Answer & get real feedback
Wrong answers come with a plain-language explanation of exactly where your reasoning went off — not just a red X. Right answers get reinforced so they stick.
3
Watch your weak spots shrink
Every session, PrepMate leans harder into the topics you're still shaky on, and eases off the ones you've mastered — so no session feels like a repeat.
4
Track your progress over time
A simple dashboard shows exactly which topics are improving, which are stuck, and what to focus on before your next study session.
